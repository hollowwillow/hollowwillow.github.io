From Pre-k through 6th grade, I won many student of the month awards. I also helped run a mini cafe during fourth grade. There were many stations such as chips, beverages, a mini starbucks, and a bakesale all at once. During 6th grade, I joined battle of the books. Although we weren't even close to winning, I still see it as an accomplishment as it was the first club that got me into reading a lot of books. I also won top 100 for a writing contest called Do the Write Thing (DTWT). Finally, I won 1st for my school's Dia de Lost Muertos art contest on a drawing that had took me a couple hours to make.

During seventh grade, I joined my school's band and played the trumpet. I commited to learning the trumpet and eventually played at Salvation Army for a concert. I also joined my school's chess club along with volleyball club. Around this time, I had just learned how to play chess, but I was very passionate about it, playing online and with friends almost all the time. And although I wasn't the best at volleyball, I tried my best to practice everyday with friends. Lastly, I won DTWT for the second time in a row for my essay. On top of all of that, throughout all of seventh grade, I maintained straight A's.

I won first place for my school's Dia de Los Muertos art contest I helped plan my middle school-play, along with creating the video, parts of the script, and story. And although I was a busy bee, I was also awarded validectorian of my class for my attendence, high test scores, and straight-A grades. I gave my valedictorian speech during graduation.
